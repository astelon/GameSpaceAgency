# Playtest Notes

Use this file to record outcomes of blind playtests: participants, final VP totals, key balance issues, card dominance, failure frequency, and recommended changes.

Current balance focus (v0.3 — after the design-review rebalance, see docs/design_review.md):
- Commercial missions should be the main source of Credits.
- Prestige missions should be the main source of VP.
- Infrastructure missions should feel like setup turns that unlock later value instead of immediate spikes.
- Persistent assets pay by distance: Credits at Earth ZOI or closer, VP beyond. Watch whether LEO probe-farming is still attractive and whether deep-space assets get built.
- Command Turns are now Level + 1 (2/3/4). Watch round length and whether Level 3 (14 CR) is worth it.
- Staggered starting credits (5/6/7/8). Watch whether the first player still wins the round-1 mission race.
- Conditional activations (Sensor Array in storms, Science Module beyond Moon Orbit). Watch whether they get used or forgotten.
- Mission Sweep in Maintenance. Watch whether good missions get swept too fast.
- Space Telescope pays 1 VP/round at GEO — the one premium exception to distance-scaled income. If it dominates, move its deploy requirement to Moon Orbit or beyond.
- Parachute-type Reentry cards are now Earth-only with a 1-Credit recovery payout. Watch whether the Earth-only limit reads clearly and whether the payout makes them attractive vs Heat Shields.
- Hybrid Cycle (+1 Thrust with a Cryogenic tank). Watch whether the cryo combo ever gets built.
- Component deck scaling (143/114/91 for 4/3/2 players). Watch whether 2p games still surface Station Hubs and Hydrogen Cores often enough.

Suggested metrics to record:
- Average VP per player
- Average launches attempted per player
- Failure rate per launch (%)
- Most-used cards and win correlation
- Observed pain points (long turns, unclear text, broken combos)
- Credits earned from Commercial missions vs other sources
- VP earned from Prestige missions vs milestones and research activations
- Number of station or satellite activations per round
- Whether players understood the split between Thrust as launch capability and Range as orbital travel budget
- VP earned per path: missions vs asset income vs milestones vs conditional activations vs end-game infrastructure bonus
- Round in which Tier 2 and Tier 3 unlocked; whether Tier 3 missions were ever completed
- How many missions were removed by the Mission Sweep and whether any were mourned
- Actions "wasted" per player (acquired cards never launched, etc.)

Suggested playtest questions:
- Did Commercial missions feel necessary but not dominant?
- Did Prestige missions feel tempting enough to delay cashflow?
- Did Infrastructure missions create useful long-term value, or did they feel too slow?
- Did station operations or research assets snowball once established?
- Was the Launch Capability Check easier to understand than the previous Lift Check wording?
